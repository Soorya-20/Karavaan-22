About Us

Karavaan is the annual cultural fest organised by the students of IISER, Pune. This yearly event features various performances by famous artists as well as students. Apart from the mainstage events that last three days, it boasts a string of exciting cultural, scientific, and social events peppered through the preceding month. Karavaan collaborates with various social initiatives including, but not limited to, the river cleaning drive, pride march, and dog vaccination and awareness campaigns.
The main events draws a large and diverse crowd from all over the city over the course of three days. Karavaan also features Band Wars and Nupura - inter-college music and dance titles vied by students all over the country.
